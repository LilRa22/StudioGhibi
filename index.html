Founded in 1985 by Isao Takahata and Hayao Miyazaki, Studio Ghibi has produced 22 acclaimed films. Ghibi's works earned global praise, with awards like the 2002 academy awards for Best Animaed Feature. The Ghibi Museum opened in 2001. Recent films like THE WIND RISES (2013) and THE RED TURTLE (2016) secured consecutive Academy Award nominations. The studio is actively working on new productions.
